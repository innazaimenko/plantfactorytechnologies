About

Our journey started back in 2016, when the founder, Dennis Usik, was developing a new cultivation technology in the laboratory. In the next three years, we have scaled the systems and the equipment allowing us to come up with a first commercial production and a valid business model. We have partnered with several supplying companies and scaled the team to 5 people full time. The next two years were on the development of digitalization and automation. This resulted in adding 4 commercial production facilities to our site. We have also built own R&D site and partnered with academia in the areas of robotics and automation. We have established the customer base and made important connections in Singapore, India, USA, Switzerland. We have established two additional production sites in Serbia and Vietnam. Our team had grown to 85 people with an expertise from plant biology, automation, to finance and strategic partnerships. The future is bright and we look forward to shaping it together with our partners.
